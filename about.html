Our Story

Founded in 2014 by Ahmed Al Rashid, BNZ Customize began as a small workshop in Dubai with a simple vision: to provide the highest quality vehicle customization services in the UAE. What started as a passion for automotive aesthetics has grown into the region's most trusted car wrapping and protection specialist.

Our journey began when Ahmed, a certified automotive technician with over 12 years of experience, noticed the lack of premium car wrapping services in Dubai. He invested in the latest equipment and partnered with industry leaders like 3M and Avery Dennison to bring world-class materials and techniques to the UAE market.

Today, we operate state-of-the-art facilities in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah, serving customers across all seven emirates. Our team of certified technicians has completed over 500 projects, from simple color changes to complex custom designs and full paint protection installations.
